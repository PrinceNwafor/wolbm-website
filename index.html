Our Heart

A Ministry That
Transforms Lives

Word of Life Bible Ministry (WOLBM) was founded in 2004 under the prophetic leadership of Prophet Dr. Ige Kelly Osaigbovo, beginning with a small prayer group and growing into a powerful, multi-national ministry.

We exist to preach the gospel to the castoffs, society's misfits, and the needy — reaching every person with the life-changing Word of God and demonstrating His power through healing, deliverance, and prophetic ministry.

Vision & Purpose

Prophet Dr. Ige Kelly Osaigbovo is an Evangelist, Teacher, and Prophet called by God in 2004 to minister the Word with power, signs, and wonders.

Starting from a prayer group in his living room, his ministry has expanded across multiple nations, transforming thousands of lives through prophetic declarations, healing services, and the uncompromising Word of God.

His mission is clear: to preach the gospel to those society has overlooked — and to demonstrate the power of God that changes destinies.
