When You're Hurting

God Is Your Healer

Whether the wound is physical, emotional, or spiritual — God's healing power is available to you today. He does not stand at a distance from your pain; He enters it with you.

WOLBM regularly holds healing services where we pray for the sick and believe God for miracles. Prophet Ige Kelly's ministry has seen countless testimonies of God's supernatural healing power.

"Lord, I come to You carrying this pain. I believe that by the stripes of Jesus I am healed. I declare that no weapon formed against my health shall prosper. Restore me, Lord, body, soul, and spirit. I receive Your healing now, in Jesus' name. Amen."

Physical healing from sickness or disease
Healing from emotional wounds and trauma
Recovery and restoration after loss
Healing in a relationship
Request Prayer for Healing
When You're Overwhelmed

He Gives Rest to the Weary

When the weight of life becomes too heavy to carry, Jesus offers an exchange — your burden for His rest. You were not designed to carry everything alone.

Cast all your anxiety on Him — because He cares for you (1 Peter 5:7). Come to a Tuesday revival service and lay your burdens before God in His presence.

"Jesus, I am tired and overwhelmed. I cannot carry this anymore in my own strength. I give You my burdens right now — the worries, the pressure, the fear of the future. I receive Your peace that passes all understanding. In Jesus' name. Amen."

When You Feel Lost

Your Purpose Is Not Lost — It's Hidden in God

Feeling directionless is not the end of your story. God has plans for you — plans for good, not for evil. The prophetic ministry at WOLBM has helped thousands discover and align with their divine purpose.

Prophet Ige Kelly's prophetic ministry is especially focused on helping people find God's direction for their lives. Book a prophetic ministry appointment or attend a service and believe God for a specific word.

"Father, I feel lost and without direction. But Your Word says You have plans for me — plans to give me hope and a future. Speak to me, Lord. Direct my steps. I surrender my path to You and trust that You will make it clear. In Jesus' name. Amen."
