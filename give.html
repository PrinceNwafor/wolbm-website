Generosity & Giving

Sow a Seed,

Your gift is more than a donation — it is a seed sown into God's work that will bear fruit for generations.

Why Give

Your Generosity
Changes Destinies

Every gift to Word of Life Bible Ministry goes directly toward advancing the gospel, supporting the poor, funding outreach missions, and operating the ministry programs that transform lives every week.

We believe in biblical generosity — that as you give, God opens doors, blesses your life, and uses your seed to bless others. Giving is not an obligation; it is an opportunity to participate in something eternal.

No gift is too small. Every contribution — whether $10 or $1,000 — is received with gratitude and stewardship.
