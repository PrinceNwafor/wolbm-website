Plan Your Visit
to WOLBM

Coming for the first time? Here's everything you need to know to make your visit comfortable and life-changing.

What to Expect

A Welcome Unlike
Any Other

1
Arrive & Be Greeted

Our friendly greeters are positioned at the entrance to welcome you by name (if you've filled out the visit form). You'll feel at home the moment you walk in.

2
Spirit-Filled Praise & Worship

Our Sunday services open with powerful, anointed worship that invites the presence of God into the room. Come ready to encounter God.

3
Prophetic Word & Preaching

Prophet Dr. Ige Kelly Osaigbovo delivers an uncompromising, spirit-filled message from the Word of God. Expect the sermon to speak directly to where you are in life.

4
Altar Call & Ministry Time

No pressure — but if you feel led, you can respond to an altar call for salvation, healing, or prayer. Our ministry team is there to pray with you personally.

5
Fellowship & Connection

After service, take time to connect with our warm congregation. You'll be introduced to ministry leaders and find out how you can get more involved.
