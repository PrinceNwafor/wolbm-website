Serving Together

Our Ministries

Every ministry arm of WOLBM exists to reach, heal, and empower people in the name of Jesus Christ.

Get Involved

Find Your Place to Serve

WOLBM has a place for everyone. Whether you're drawn to worship, prayer, outreach, or youth — there is a ministry for you.

🔥
Power Must Change Hands — Tuesday Revival

Our flagship Tuesday revival meetings where the Holy Spirit moves in power. Expect healing, deliverance, prophetic ministry, and transformation every week.

📻
Radio Ministry

The "Power Must Change Hands Radio Program" extends our reach far beyond the walls of the church, bringing prophetic words and the gospel to thousands of listeners.

❤️
Care Ministry

We believe the gospel is both preached and demonstrated. Our Care Ministry supports community members in practical need — food, counseling, and assistance.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑
Youth Empowerment

We are raising the next generation of believers, leaders, and world-changers. Our youth programs offer discipleship, fellowship, and real biblical training for young people.

⛓️
Prison Ministry

God's love knows no boundaries — not even prison walls. Our Prison Ministry brings the transforming Word of God to incarcerated individuals and their families.

📖
Sunday School

Our Sunday School ministry provides solid biblical education for children and youth, building a strong foundation of faith from an early age.

👶
Children's Ministry

Children are not the church of tomorrow — they are the church of today. Our children's programs are engaging, Spirit-filled, and age-appropriate.

🌍
Missions & Outreach

The gospel must go to every nation. WOLBM is actively involved in local and international outreach, spreading the love and Word of God across borders.

🎵
Worship Ministry

Our worship team leads the congregation into the presence of God each Sunday through anointed, spirit-led praise and worship that ushers in God's glory.
